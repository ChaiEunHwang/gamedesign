Conditional Design

Pixel Drawing

The design was very smooth and minimalistic, with snugly, comfortable oversized ear covers. The hanger is wrapped with smooth, cushiony padding without looking bulky, and connects to the headphone parts seamlessly. It's in a muted greyish, oatmeal color with matte finish.
